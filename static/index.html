How it works

Four steps for a Field Level Worker during animal registration

1
📷
Capture photo
FLW takes a photo of the animal — any lighting, background, or pose supported.
2
🤖
AI analysis
Python backend runs ResNet-50 ONNX model — extracts 2048-dim feature vectors and computes cosine similarity.
3
📊
Breed suggestion
Top breed + confidence score returned via API. FLW accepts or overrides with manual entry.
4
✅
Register & submit
Verified breed auto-fills BPA registration form, reducing errors and improving data integrity.
